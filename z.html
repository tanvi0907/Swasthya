About Swasthya Hospital

The premier multi-specialty tertiary care hospital of Vapi has been acknowledged as a centre of medical excellence. The kind of confidence, trust it has developed on strong foundation of the state-of-the-art facilities, best medical expertise, research and educational endeavors; that today it serves patients from all walks of life.
Based on a powerful Sanskrit inspiration, “Sarvetra Sukhina:Santu, Sarve SantuNiramaya:” which means “Let all be blissful, Let all stay healthy”, the hospital has focused its operation on providing quality care with a human touch; which truly reflects the essence of its motto “The Greatest Wealth is Health”.
The hospital is located on the Mumbai - Ahmedabad highway (NH - 8) at Vapi. The hospital started functioning in 2007 with 45 beds and 30 Specialists (Full time & Visiting), with one of the largest Intensive Care Units (ICUs), most advanced Operation Theaters, and manpower of nearly 150. Hospital attended to around 1500 In-patients and 20000 Out-patients in the last one year. Easily Accessible, located on National Highway, 45 bedded central air conditioned indoor facility, 10 bedded fully equipped ultramodern ICU, 2 bedded isolated burn ward, 8 bedded General Ward (4 Male / 4 Female), 4 Semi Deluxe beds, 12 Deluxe Rooms, 4 Suite Rooms, Modular Operation Theatre with Laminar Air flow, Ultramodern Cath Lab “Schiller Make”, Haemodialysis, 16 slice CT machine, Fully equipped pathology laboratory, 24 hrs. in-house Pharmacy, 24 hrs. Ambulance Service
