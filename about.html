Our hospital's major aim is to prioritize health over everything. We just want to make people realise that how they are themselves destroying their lives.

Swasthya Hospital is a Millennium dream institute started as a multispecialty hospital in GIDC, Vapi; now entering its 20th Year of Operations in a newly renovated and rejuvenated form. It caters to people of all social and economic strata covering the entire Gujarat, North Maharashtra, and neighboring union territories of Daman & Silvassa.

Apart from routine medical specialties, it also has (1) Advance Obgyn & Infertility IVF-ICSI Unit, (2) Total Onco & Cancer Care with Laparoscopic surgeries, (3) Dermatology & Advanced Cosmetology and (4) Cardiology Department with facilities for angiography & angioplasties with 14 ICU beds. These super specialties also attract patients from all over India & abroad.
